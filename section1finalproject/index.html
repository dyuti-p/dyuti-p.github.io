"so there!"

SoThere.com was founded in 1997 as a sanctuary for the thoughts that were left unsaid. In search of closure, users submitted letters, accounts of grievances, heartbreak, and revelations. With help of the Wayback machine, I pulled 28 letters its archive, dating to February 2003, the month of my birth. I was born on the 28th day. These letters document all these people across the globe who were navigating their fleshed out lives while I was just learning how to survive outside the womb.
