Assignment Guidelines

OVERVIEW
Students will create an online publication (fan site, zine, exhibition, etc) that includes 3-5 separate pages and a navigational tool/toolset. No single page and its linked content can be larger than 5 megabytes.

GOALS
Choose a topic for your publication, and establish a consistent visual style to deliver uniform messaging and theming (10 pts)
Interpret the webpage format to creatively present your topic's information to a viewer (10 pts)
What is the flow of information (steady or dynamic)? What is your navigation system between pages? Demonstrate intentional strategies to guide a viewer through your pages by exploring the principles of sequence: cadence, rhythm, pause, punctuation and irregularity, logical order (or intentional disorder), etc. (10 pts)
At least one page of your publication must utilize a grid: flexbox, floats, or tables (10 pts)
Organize your files and directories by instituting and delivering an effective, logical schema (5 pts)
Direct the class through your intended site navigation during critique (5 pts)
