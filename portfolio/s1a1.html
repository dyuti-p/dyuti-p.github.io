Assignment Guidelines

OVERVIEW
In small groups of 2 or 3, students will work together to establish a system that individual students will then utilize to recreate an image with Binary, ASCII, or Unicode characters. Groups may reconvene to reassess their group’s systems or individual students may “fork the code” (within reason) to better serve their images. Final text images must be a minimum of 60 character rows/columns along the shortest edge.

GOALS
Become familiar with a sandbox development environment Explore the foundation and utility of systems and languages In a group, develop an adequate system that can be used to clearly and/or creatively communicate your groups’ images (10 pts) Optimize your system for your individual image (10 pts) In critique, clearly discuss and communicate your developed system as related to your text drawing (5 pts) Start exploring inline styling and spans (Extra: +1-5 points)
